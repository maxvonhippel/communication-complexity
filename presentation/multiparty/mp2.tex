\begin{example}[Majority Parity]
\(\textsc{MajPar} : \mathbb{B}^n \times \mathbb{B}^n \times \mathbb{B}^n \to \mathbb{B}\)
is precisely
\(\langle x_{1}, x_{2}, x_{3} \rangle \mapsto 1\)
if $\bigoplus_{i = 1}^{n} \text{majority}(x_{1i}, x_{2i}, x_{3i})$ else 0.
\end{example}
