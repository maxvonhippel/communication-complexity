\begin{itemize}
	\item You're allowed to be wrong sometimes.
	\item You have a lava lamp or a coin or something.
	\item Instead of $C(f)$ the complexity of $f$,
	we discuss $R(f) = \mathbb{E}[C(f)]$ the \emph{expected}
	complexity of $f$.
\end{itemize}
